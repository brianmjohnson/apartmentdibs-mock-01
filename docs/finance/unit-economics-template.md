## Appendix: Calculation Definitions

### MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
Sum of all monthly subscription revenue normalized to a monthly amount.

### ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)
MRR × 12 (annualized monthly recurring revenue)

### ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
Total MRR / Total active customers

### CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
(Sales expenses + Marketing expenses) / Number of new customers acquired

### LTV (Customer Lifetime Value)
ARPU × Gross margin % × (1 / Monthly churn rate)

### Churn Rate
(Customers lost in period / Customers at start of period) × 100

### Net Revenue Retention
((MRR at start + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) / MRR at start) × 100
